Welcome To the Quiz Management System!!
When You open the program, It will show a list of questions in the listbox.
You have the options to delete,add and update items within the textfile.(Warning: You must keep the cursor at the selected item in order to update or delete the item.
Press on an item two times to edit or delete.Otherwise you can insert a question or answer within the entry box. 
On the top left corner, press the File option, and you choose to take a quiz or read the "README" file. For each question,
it is assigned random points from 1 to 3 each time you take it. There is also a search selection where you can search for any question
within your file.
